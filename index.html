Antigorithm

Antigorithm enables users to customize their video feeds. Instead of having a feed that only sometimes shows you what you actually want to watch, You can customize your feed to always show latest videos from specific channels.

Below you can generate your feed. select all channels you want in your feed and click "Go To Feed". This will open your customized feed. That feed will show the 15 latest videos from each of the selected channels. You can than bookmark your feed to always have it nearby.

After checking the checkbox for each channel you want, scroll down to the bottom to find the "Go To Feed" button
